Dice Game

How to Play

This is a dice guessing game where you try to predict the outcome of a dice roll!

  • Pick a number from 1 to 6
  • Click ROLL to roll the dice
  • Win: Guess correctly and earn +10 points
  • Lose: Guess wrong and lose -2 points

Track your score, wins, and losses as you play. Use RESET to start over!
